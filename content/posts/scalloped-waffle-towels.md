## Scalloped vs. waffle

**Scalloped-edge** towels are about the pretty detail — lovely as display hand towels by the sink. **Waffle** weave is about texture and fast drying — great for bath towels in a small, humid cottage bathroom. Mix both: waffle bath sheets, scalloped hand towels.

## Turkish cotton, explained

Turkish (Peshtemal) towels are flatter and lighter than fluffy terry — they dry quicker, take up less space, and soften beautifully with each wash. The trade-off is less plush cushion, which most cottage bathrooms happily accept for the look.

## FAQ

**Do waffle towels actually dry you well?**
Yes — the waffle pockets give plenty of surface area; they feel thinner but absorb well and dry much faster than terry.

**How do I keep them soft?**
Wash without fabric softener (it coats the fibers and reduces absorbency); a little vinegar in the rinse keeps them fresh and fluffy.
